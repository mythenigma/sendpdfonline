Grabify IP Logger

Grabify is a free and simple tool that allows you to track IP addresses and determine the geographic location of visitors. While other similar tools exist, Grabify is popular for its simplicity and ease of use. As of now, it has over 66 million IP logs in its database.

How to Track Someone's Location Using Grabify

To track someone's IP address using Grabify, follow these simple steps:

  • 1. Find a link to an interesting topic or website that your target would be tempted to click.
  • 2. Visit the Grabify website and paste the link into the "Enter URL or tracking code" box on the homepage.
  • 3. Click "Create URL". You'll be asked to agree to the terms of use. Once agreed, you'll be taken to a page with more information.

Interesting Email Tracker

Tracking pixels (also called web beacons, web bugs, or page tags) are tiny 1x1 pixel images embedded in emails or webpages to track user actions. These pixels are usually invisible and provide valuable data to marketers about user engagement and behavior.

In email marketing, this technique is known as "open tracking". It helps you monitor when and how many times an email is opened and can provide insights into how recipients interact with the email content.
