PDF View Limits (Open Count Limits)

Published: January 15, 2025 | Updated: December 03, 2025

Welcome to MaiPDF, your trusted solution for secure and controlled document sharing. Instead of date-based expiration, you can manage access using view count limits and revocable links.

Why Use View Limits for PDFs?

View limits help ensure your documents are only accessible a limited number of times. This works well for sensitive information, limited previews, or controlled sharing.

MaiPDF View Limit Features

MaiPDF provides practical options for controlling access, including:

  • Customizable view count limits
  • Automatic access blocking after the limit is reached
  • Manual link revocation at any time
  • Works alongside passwords, watermarks, and access tracking

How to Set View Limits with MaiPDF

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF.
  2. Enable view limits in the sharing settings.
  3. Choose the maximum number of opens/views allowed.
  4. Share your document securely with your audience.

Benefits of Using View Limits

  • Maintain control over shared documents.
  • Enhance security by limiting repeated access.
  • Ensure compliance with data retention policies.
