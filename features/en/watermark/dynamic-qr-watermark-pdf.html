Introducing Dynamic QR Watermarks

PDF documents are the standard for sharing important information, but this also presents challenges for protecting sensitive content. MaiPDF addresses this concern with our innovative Dynamic QR Watermark technology.

Our watermarking system creates a unique digital fingerprint on your documents that:

  • Tracks the source of any unauthorized distribution
  • Changes dynamically with each viewer and session
  • Preserves document readability while maintaining security
  • Works invisibly in the background to protect your content

How Dynamic QR Watermarks Work

Our dynamic QR watermark technology embeds a unique QR code on each page of your PDF document. Unlike static watermarks, our solution creates a different watermark for each viewer and viewing session.

When someone accesses your document:

  1. The system generates a unique QR code containing viewer information (device, IP address, time)
  2. This code is embedded into the document in real-time
  3. If the document is shared without permission, the QR code reveals exactly who leaked it
  4. Even screenshots or photographs of the document will contain the identifying watermark
