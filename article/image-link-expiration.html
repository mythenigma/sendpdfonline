Image Link Expiration

Share images that close automatically after a set time.

What is image link expiration?

It’s a built‑in time limit. Once the deadline hits, the link stops working on its own.

When to use it

  • Short client review windows
  • Limited‑time campaign assets
  • Internal approvals with firm deadlines

How to set expiration

  1. Upload your images.
  2. Pick an expiration time (hours or days).
  3. Share the link.
