Benefits of Watermarking

Deter Unauthorized Sharing

When viewers know their identity is embedded in the document, they're much less likely to share it without permission.

Trace Leak Sources

If your document ends up in the wrong hands, the watermark reveals exactly who shared it and when.

Legal Protection

Watermarks provide evidence of ownership and can support legal action against copyright infringement.

Screenshot Protection

Even if someone takes a screenshot, your watermark remains visible, protecting your content.

Customizable Appearance

Adjust watermark opacity, position, size, and color to balance visibility with readability.

Dynamic Updates

Watermarks are generated in real-time, showing accurate viewer information for each access.
