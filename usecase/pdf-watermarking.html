What is PDF Watermarking?

PDF watermarking is a method of embedding visible or invisible marks on your PDF documents to indicate ownership, enhance security, and prevent unauthorized use. MaiPDF offers advanced dynamic watermarking capabilities that adapt to your needs.

Security

  • Prevent unauthorized sharing
  • Track document usage

Branding

  • Add custom watermarks
  • Enhance document credibility

Tracking

  • Monitor document access
  • Track user information
  • Analyze usage patterns

How to Use Watermarking

  2. Choose your watermark type and customize it
  3. Apply the watermark and download your protected document
