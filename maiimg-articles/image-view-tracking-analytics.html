How I Use Analytics

Client Follow-ups

I share portfolio links with potential clients. If they view it 5+ times, I know they're interested and follow up within 24 hours. This has increased my conversion rate by 40%.

Content Strategy

I track which image collections get the most views. This helps me understand what clients want to see and create more of that content.

Timing Optimization

I noticed my event photo galleries get most views on weekends. Now I share them Friday evening for maximum engagement.

Clean Up Old Links

If a link hasn't been viewed in 30 days, I revoke it. Keeps my dashboard clean and prevents outdated content from being accessed.
