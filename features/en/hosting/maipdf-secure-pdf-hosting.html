Upload & Configure

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Upload Your PDF

Visit maipdf.com and drag your PDF file onto the upload area. A unique sharing link is generated immediately after upload completes.

No registration required Any file size
2

Configure Link Restrictions

Set any combination of these controls:

Expiration Date — auto-disable after a specific date
View Limit — max number of opens allowed
Download Prevention — view-only mode
Print Restrictions — disable printing
Password — require password to view
Watermark — viewer identity overlay
3

Share & Monitor

Copy the secure link or download the QR code. Share via email, messaging, or embed on a website. Use the analytics dashboard to monitor who accessed your document, when, and from where.
