Why Secure PDF Sharing Matters

In today's digital world, PDF documents often contain sensitive information that needs protection. Whether you're sharing business proposals, legal documents, or confidential reports, ensuring the security of your PDFs is crucial.

Key Security Challenges

  • Unauthorized access to sensitive information
  • Document copying and distribution
  • Loss of control over shared documents
  • Inability to track document usage
