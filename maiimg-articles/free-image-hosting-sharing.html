Pro Tips I've Learned

Image Optimization

For web sharing, compress images before uploading. Smaller files = faster loading. I use 80% quality JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics.

QR Code Placement

Make QR codes at least 2cm x 2cm for easy scanning. Test with multiple phones before printing. High contrast (black on white) works best.

Use View Tracking

Check your dashboard regularly. If someone views your link 10+ times, they're probably interested. Perfect time to follow up.

Clean Up Old Links

Revoke old links you don't need anymore. Keeps your dashboard clean and prevents outdated images from being accessed.
