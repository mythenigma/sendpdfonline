Enhancing Document Security with PDF View Limits

Published: January 15, 2025 | Updated: December 03, 2025

Take control of your sensitive documents with MaiPDF's advanced view limit feature

Why Set View Limits for Your PDFs?

In today's digital world, sharing documents securely is more important than ever. While PDFs are a universal format for sharing information, controlling how many times they can be accessed adds an essential layer of security. MaiPDF's view limit feature gives you precise control over document access, ensuring your sensitive information stays protected.

Benefits of Setting View Limits

Enhanced Security

Prevent unauthorized sharing by limiting the number of times your document can be opened

Usage Control

Review and review exactly how many times your document has been accessed

Access Management

Ensure documents are only viewed by intended recipients the specified number of times

How View Limits Work

MaiPDF's view limit feature is simple yet powerful. When sharing a PDF, you can set a specific number of times it can be opened. Once this limit is reached, the document becomes inaccessible. This feature is particularly useful for:

  • Sensitive business proposals
  • Confidential legal documents
  • Limited-time promotional materials
  • Academic test materials
  • Time-sensitive financial reports

Setting Up View Limits

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF's secure platform
  2. Choose your desired number of views (from 1 to 10,000)
  3. Configure additional security settings if needed
  4. Generate a secure sharing link
  5. Share with your intended recipients

Pro Tip

For unlimited views, set the view count to a high number (e.g., 999999). The system will automatically assign 10 views if left blank.

Additional Security Features

View limits can be combined with other MaiPDF security features for comprehensive document protection:

  • Password protection
  • Download restrictions
  • Print limitations
  • Dynamic watermarks
  • access analytics

Take control of your PDF sharing with MaiPDF's powerful view limit feature. Whether you're sharing sensitive business documents or controlling access to confidential information, view limits provide an extra layer of security you can count on.
