Why QR works for image sets

QR codes remove the friction of typing long URLs. Readers scan, land on a gallery, and browse in a single flow.

Where to place QR codes

Retail & campaigns

Use shelf cards, brand displays, and promo posters to point visitors to a gallery.

Business cards

Printed follow-ups

Pro tips

Choose a readable size

Test a few phones. In many cases, larger QR codes print better and scan faster.

Avoid blur and glare

Use matte paper when possible, and keep the QR area clear of heavy shadows.

Revoke when needed

When a campaign ends, disable the link from the dashboard so the QR no longer grants access.

Add a short CTA

A simple instruction helps readers scan immediately, especially at distance.
