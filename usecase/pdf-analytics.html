What is PDF Analytics?

PDF analytics shows you what happens after you share a file. With MaiPDF, you can see access trends, reading behavior, and engagement details in one clear dashboard.

Key Analytics Features

View Tracking

  • Total views across all shares
  • Unique readers vs. repeat visits
  • Time spent on each document

Download Analytics

  • Total download volume
  • Where downloads came from
  • Download timing and spikes

Advanced Analytics

  • Reading behavior insights
  • Location-based trends
  • Device and browser breakdowns

How to Use PDF Analytics

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF
  2. Turn on analytics for the file
  3. Review the reports and key insights
