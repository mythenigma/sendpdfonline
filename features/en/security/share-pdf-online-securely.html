Key Features

Full Control Over Every Shared PDF

No-Download Viewer

Recipients view in the browser without a download or save button, so the original file never leaves your control.

Expiration Dates

Set a date or countdown timer. Once expired, the link stops working automatically — no manual revocation needed.

View Limits

Cap total opens per link so shared URLs cannot be redistributed and reused beyond your intended audience.

Password Protection

Require a password before the document can be viewed, adding a gate even if the link is accidentally forwarded.

Email Verification

Require viewers to verify their email address before access, so you know exactly who opened the document.

Dynamic Watermark

Overlay viewer-specific details like email, IP, and timestamp on every page to deter leaks and trace any capture.

Telegram Alerts

Get instant notifications on Telegram whenever someone opens your shared PDF, with device and location info.

Access Audit Log

Review a full history of opens with timestamps, IP addresses, device types, and geographic locations.

Why It Matters

Email Attachments Aren't Secure

Once you email a PDF, you lose all control. Anyone can forward it, download it, or print unlimited copies. MaiPDF replaces attachments with a controlled viewer link that enforces your security policy in the browser.

  • Revoke access at any time with one click
  • Works on every device and browser
