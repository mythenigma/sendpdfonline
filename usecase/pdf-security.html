What is PDF Security?

PDF security refers to the measures taken to protect PDF documents from unauthorized access, modification, and distribution. MaiPDF provides comprehensive security features to ensure your documents remain safe and confidential.

Key Security Features

Encryption

  • Advanced encryption standards
  • Password protection
  • Secure key management

Access Control

  • User-based permissions
  • Time-based access
  • IP-based restrictions

Watermarking

  • Dynamic watermarks
  • User-specific marks
  • Timestamp tracking

How to Secure Your PDFs

  1. Upload your PDF document to MaiPDF
  2. Choose your security settings and preferences
  3. Apply security measures and download your protected document
