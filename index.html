Choose the right level

Browser link first. App DRM for high-risk files.

SendPDFOnline is the fast path: upload once, share a link or QR code, and manage online access. But a browser cannot fully control operating-system screenshots or copied local files.

For paid course files, confidential decks, contracts, or documents that need device binding and revocation after distribution, use MaiPDF App DRM and protected .maipdf files.
