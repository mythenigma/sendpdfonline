What Is PDF Link Sharing?

PDF link sharing gives your file a unique URL. Anyone with that link can open the PDF directly in their browser — no extra software needed. You control whether recipients can download, print, or only view online, and you can set view limits, expiration dates, and track who opens the file.

Checklist

Before & After You Share

Before Sharing

  • Finalize the document — remove drafts and old pages.
  • Decide on access level: public, password-protected, or email-verified.
  • Set an expiration date for time-sensitive content.
  • Enable no-download / no-print for confidential files.
  • Add a watermark for high-value documents.

After Sharing

  • Check access records within 24 hours to confirm key recipients opened.
  • Follow up with contacts who viewed but haven't responded.
  • Tighten permissions if the link gets forwarded unexpectedly.
  • Use "replace file" to update content without changing the URL.
  • Disable old links once they're no longer needed.
