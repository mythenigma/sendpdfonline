PDF Expiration Dates

Welcome to MaiPDF, your trusted solution for secure and controlled document sharing. Learn how to set expiration dates for your PDFs to manage access effectively.

Why Use Expiration Dates for PDFs?

Setting expiration dates ensures that your documents are only accessible for a specific period. This is ideal for time-sensitive information, limited offers, or compliance requirements.

MaiPDF's Expiration Date Features

MaiPDF provides advanced options for setting expiration dates, including:

  • Customizable expiration periods
  • Automatic access revocation
  • Notifications for upcoming expirations
  • Integration with other security features

How to Set Expiration Dates with MaiPDF

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF.
  2. Enable the expiration date feature in the settings.
  3. Choose a specific date or duration for access.
  4. Share your document securely with your audience.

Benefits of Using Expiration Dates

  • Maintain control over time-sensitive documents.
  • Enhance security by limiting access duration.
  • Ensure compliance with data retention policies.
