What is Expiring a File?

MaiPDF's Expiring a File feature gives you unprecedented control over your documents. Set specific view limits for your PDF files, after which the links automatically expire and become inaccessible.

Unlike traditional PDF sharing methods, MaiPDF's expiration system ensures:

  • Complete control over your sensitive information
  • Ability to modify or revoke access at any time
  • Detailed tracking of document views and access attempts
  • Secure sharing without worrying about unauthorized redistribution

How Does Expiring a File Work?

When you generate a link for your PDF using MaiPDF, you can set precise limits on document access. The system provides multiple layers of protection:

Set View Limits

Define exactly how many times your PDF can be opened before the link automatically expires. Once your set view limit is reached, the document becomes inaccessible.

Modify Link Settings

Even after sending the PDF link, you maintain complete control. Change access settings, update view limits, or completely revoke access at any time from your dashboard.

Track Every View

Receive real-time notifications when your document is accessed. View detailed analytics about who opened your file, when they accessed it, and from which device.
