Analytics Features

Everything Your Dashboard Tracks

Real-Time Tracking

Every PDF open is logged the instant it happens. No delays, no batching — see activity as it occurs.

View Counts

See exactly how many times each document has been opened, broken down by unique viewers and total opens.

Timestamps

Every access event includes a precise date and time, so you know exactly when your PDF was viewed.

Device Info

Identify the browser, operating system, and device type of every viewer — desktop, tablet, or mobile.

Telegram Alerts

Get instant push notifications on Telegram the moment someone opens your PDF, including viewer details.

CSV Export

Download all access records as a CSV file for offline analysis, compliance reporting, or CRM integration.

Email Verify Gate

Require viewers to verify their email before accessing the PDF, linking every open to a verified identity.

Dashboard Overview

A single page showing all your shared PDFs, total views, recent activity, and quick links to detailed logs.

Access Records

Detailed Viewer Logs

Every time someone opens your PDF, a new row appears in your access records. You can filter by date, export to CSV, or check individual viewer details at a glance.

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser and OS fingerprint
  • Verified email (if gate is enabled)
  • One-click CSV download
