Best Practices

Watermarking Best Practices for Designers

Watermark Placement

  • Position watermarks over key design elements so they cannot be easily cropped out
  • Use semi-transparent overlays that don't obscure the work
  • Adapt watermark density to the type of asset — subtle for presentations, more prominent for high-value originals
  • Include your studio name or contact information

Security Settings

  • Set view limits for sensitive or early-stage projects
  • Enable no-download and no-print for all client-facing shares
  • Add expiration dates to temporary or proposal-stage access
  • Use passwords for confidential or NDA-covered work

Brand Protection

  • Use consistent watermark styles across all client deliverables
  • Include your brand mark or logo in the watermark text
  • Create different protection levels for drafts vs. finals
  • Maintain a sharing log so you always know who received what
