Why Limit PDF Open Times?

Controlling the number of times a PDF can be opened is crucial for protecting sensitive information and maintaining document security. This feature is particularly useful for:

  • One-time access documents
  • Confidential business materials
  • Limited distribution content
  • Exclusive offers or promotions

How to Set PDF Open Limits

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF
  2. Set the maximum number of opens (e.g., 3 times)
  3. Configure additional security settings
  4. Generate and share the secure link

Benefits of Open Limits

Enhanced Security

  • Prevents unlimited access
  • Controls document distribution
  • Reduces unauthorized sharing

Usage Control

  • Track access attempts
  • Monitor open counts
  • Manage document lifecycle

Protection

  • Prevent unauthorized copying
  • Control document sharing
  • Maintain content integrity
