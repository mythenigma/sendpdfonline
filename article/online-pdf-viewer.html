Why Use an Online PDF Viewer?

Sending a link instead of an attachment means everyone sees the same version, you can update the file without resending, and you can add access controls. The viewer runs in the browser on desktop and mobile—no install, no plugin.

What Users Can Do in the Viewer

Read in browser

No local app or plugin. Works in Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, and mobile browsers.

Navigate & zoom

Page controls, zoom in/out, and scroll. Same experience on phone and desktop.

Secure access

Optional email verification, view limits, and expiry. Owner can track who opened.
