Sales Pitch Deck Security

Share pitch decks with prospects without worrying about unauthorized forwarding. Control access, track views, and protect proprietary pricing.

Sales pitch decks contain proprietary pricing, competitive positioning, and strategic insights. When shared via email or cloud links, they can be forwarded to competitors in seconds. MaiPDF lets you share pitch decks through secure links with full control over who views them, how many times, and for how long — while tracking every open in real time.

Security Features

Protect Every Aspect of Your Pitch Deck

View Limits

Set exactly how many times a prospect can open your deck — 1, 3, or 5 views. Once reached, access is automatically revoked.

Password Protection

Require a password before viewing. Share the password separately through a secure channel to ensure only the intended recipient can access it.

Dynamic Watermark

Overlay viewer-specific information (IP, timestamp) on every page. Deters screenshots and traces leaks back to the source.

Expiration Date

Set links to expire after your negotiation window closes. Access automatically ends on the date you choose — no manual cleanup needed.

Email Verification

Require a one-time email code before viewing. Know exactly which person opened your deck — not just which device.

Real-Time Analytics

See when your deck was opened, from which location, and on what device. Prioritize follow-ups with the most engaged prospects.

No-Download Mode

Disable download and print buttons completely. Prospects can view the deck online but cannot save a local copy to forward.

Revoke Access

Disable any link instantly from your dashboard. Even if the link was forwarded, it becomes inaccessible the moment you revoke.
