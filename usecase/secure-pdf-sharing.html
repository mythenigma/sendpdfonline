Why Secure PDF Sharing Matters

In today's digital age, sharing PDFs is a common practice, but it comes with risks. Unauthorized access, data breaches, and misuse of sensitive information are just a few of the challenges. Secure sharing ensures your documents remain protected.

MaiPDF's Security Features

  • Access control to restrict unauthorized viewing
  • Dynamic watermarks to deter misuse
  • Real-time tracking to monitor document access
  • Encryption to safeguard sensitive information
  • Password protection for document security
  • Expiration dates for temporary access
  • IP address restrictions for location control
  • Download restrictions to prevent copying

How to Share PDFs Securely

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF
  2. Set access permissions and security options
  3. Generate a secure sharing link
  4. Share the link with your intended recipients
