Internal Company Document Sharing

Share OKRs, HR policies, strategic plans, and board reports internally without data leaks. Control who sees what, track every view, and revoke access anytime.

Secure Your Docs See All Features

8 min read   March 23, 2026   Security

Every organization shares sensitive documents daily — quarterly reports, OKRs, strategic plans, HR policies, and board presentations. But the moment a file is emailed or dropped into a shared drive, control disappears. Recipients download, forward, and the document spreads far beyond its intended audience.

MaiPDF replaces file distribution with access distribution. Instead of sending a file, you share a secure, browser-only link with built-in controls: password protection, email verification, view limits, dynamic watermarks, expiration dates, and a full audit trail — all revocable in one click.

How It Works

1

3

Share & Monitor

Distribute the link or QR code to your team. Monitor views in real time and revoke access whenever needed.

Security Features

Full Control Over Every Internal Document

Password Protection

Require a password before anyone can view the document. Change it anytime without re-uploading.

Email Verification Gate

Only approved email addresses can open the document. Unauthorized viewers are blocked automatically.

View Limits

Set a maximum number of opens — 3, 10, or 50 — then the link expires automatically.

Dynamic Watermark

Overlay the viewer's IP or email on every page. Screenshots become traceable back to the source.

Expiration Dates

Set documents to expire after a specific date. Perfect for time-sensitive policies and quarterly reports.

No Download / No Print

Disable downloading and printing entirely. The document exists only in the browser — nowhere else.

Access Audit Trail

See who viewed the document, when, from which IP and device. Full compliance-ready logs.

Telegram Alerts

Receive an instant Telegram notification every time someone opens your document.
