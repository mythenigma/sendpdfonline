How MaiPDF Convert Works

Transform your PDFs to secure formats in just a few simple steps

1

Upload Your PDF

Start by uploading your PDF document to our secure platform. The process happens entirely in your browser—your files are never sent to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

MaiPDF Convert accepts PDFs of virtually any size and complexity. For optimal performance, we recommend files under 100MB, though larger documents can be processed with sufficient system memory.

2

Choose Your Output Format

Select the conversion option that best suits your security needs:

Images in ZIP

Extracts each page as an individual image file, then compresses them in a ZIP archive for easy distribution.

Imaginized PDF

Creates a PDF where each page is an image, preventing text editing while maintaining the PDF format.

Imaginized PDF with Print

Like Imaginized PDF, but with printing permission enabled for authorized sharing.

PDF with All Restrictions

Maximum security PDF with all permissions removed—cannot be edited, printed, or content copied.

3

Optional Image Compression

Toggle image compression to reduce file size while maintaining good visual quality. This is particularly useful when:

  • Sending documents as email attachments with size limitations
  • Uploading content to websites with file size restrictions
  • Optimizing for faster loading on mobile devices
  • Conserving storage space without sacrificing readability
4

Download Your Secured Document

Once processing is complete, download your converted document immediately. The entire conversion happens locally in your browser, ensuring maximum privacy and security for your sensitive information.

Your converted documents will maintain the visual fidelity of the original while adding the security benefits of image-based content protection.
