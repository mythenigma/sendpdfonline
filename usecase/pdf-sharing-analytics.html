Why Analytics Matter in PDF Sharing

Understanding how your PDFs are being shared and accessed is crucial for improving engagement, ensuring compliance, and making data-driven decisions.

MaiPDF's Analytics Features

MaiPDF provides a comprehensive suite of analytics tools, including:

  • Real-time access tracking
  • Viewer demographics and device details
  • Page-by-page engagement metrics
  • Download and sharing statistics

How to Use Analytics with MaiPDF

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF.
  2. Enable analytics tracking in the settings.
  3. Share your document securely.
  4. Monitor analytics through the MaiPDF dashboard.

Benefits of Using MaiPDF Analytics

  • Optimize your content for better engagement.
  • Ensure compliance with document access policies.
