Watermark in Action

What Viewers See

When a recipient opens your watermarked PDF, MaiPDF renders a unique overlay on every page in real time. The watermark includes a QR code that encodes the viewer's email or session identifier, their IP address, and the exact access timestamp.

Because the watermark is generated per viewer and per session, no two people ever see the same mark. If a screenshot or photograph of the document surfaces, scanning the QR code instantly reveals who accessed it, when, and from where.

You control the watermark opacity, position, and supplementary text through the MaiPDF settings panel — balancing readability with security.
