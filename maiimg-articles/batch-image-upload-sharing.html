Pro tips

Arrange the order first

Readers trust the sequence. Use your preferred ordering before publishing.

Keep files web-friendly

Smaller images load faster. Optimize for clarity, not for maximum resolution.

Test the QR size

Print big enough for common phones. Do a quick scan test before the full print run.

Follow up using opens

Spikes often indicate strong interest. Send your next message after the first batch of opens.
