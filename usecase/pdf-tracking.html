What is PDF Tracking?

PDF tracking shows you what happens after you share a file. MaiPDF lets you see views, downloads, and engagement so you can follow up with confidence.

Key Features

View Tracking

  • See total view counts
  • Understand page-by-page interest
  • Check how long readers stay

Download Analytics

  • Track download activity
  • See how often it’s shared
  • Spot engagement trends

Advanced Analytics

  • Clear, detailed reports
  • Reader engagement metrics
  • Customizable dashboards

How to Use PDF Tracking

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF
  2. Turn on tracking for the file
  3. Share your document link
  4. Watch the analytics dashboard
