How to Set Up PDF View Limits: A Simple Guide

1. Upload Your PDF

Visit MaiPDF and click the "Upload" button to add your document. It's as simple as dragging and dropping your file.

2. Set Your View Limit

Choose how many times your document can be opened. For example:

  • Set to "1" for one-time viewing (perfect for sensitive documents)
  • Choose "5" for small team reviews
  • Pick "50" for wider distribution with control
  • Use "999999" for unlimited views

3. Add Extra Protection (Optional)

Enhance your document's security with additional features:

  • Password protection - Add a secret key for access
  • Download restrictions - Prevent file saving
  • Print controls - Disable printing options
  • Dynamic watermarks - Track document origin

4. Share Your Protected PDF

Get your secure link and share it with your intended recipients. They can view the document instantly in their browser â€“ no software installation needed!
