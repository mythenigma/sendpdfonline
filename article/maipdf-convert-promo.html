Technical Details

MaiPDF uses advanced rendering technology to ensure high-fidelity conversion from PDF to image formats. You can choose from multiple quality settings:

Supported Output Formats

We support the most widely used image formats, each with its own advantages:

  • JPG: Best for photographs and complex graphics with smaller file sizes
  • PNG: Ideal for text-heavy documents and graphics with transparent backgrounds

File Organization

All converted images are organized in a clear folder structure that mirrors your original document's page order. For multi-page documents, you can choose between:

  • Individual Files: Each page is saved as a separate image file
  • ZIP Package: All images are compressed into a single downloadable archive
  • New PDF: Images can be reassembled into a new PDF file with security settings applied

FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a file size limit for PDF conversion?

No, there's no file size limit. MaiPDF can handle documents of any size, from simple one-page files to complex multi-hundred-page technical documents.

How long does the conversion process take?

The conversion time depends on the document size, but typically it takes just a few seconds. Even large documents with hundreds of pages are processed quickly thanks to our powerful backend infrastructure.

Can I convert password-protected PDFs?

Yes, you can convert password-protected PDFs. You'll need to provide the password before the conversion process can begin to ensure you have the right to access and convert the document.

Are my documents stored on your servers after conversion?

No, we don't store your documents. All files are automatically deleted from our servers 24 hours after processing. We recommend downloading your converted files immediately after conversion.

Does converting PDF to image affect quality?

No, MaiPDF uses high-fidelity rendering technology to preserve all details. You can choose from different quality settings based on your needs, from web-optimized to print-ready resolutions.

Can I convert only specific pages of a PDF?

Yes, our tool allows you to select specific page ranges for conversion. This is particularly useful when you only need to share portions of a larger document.

What happens if I lose my internet connection during conversion?

If you lose connectivity during conversion, simply re-upload your file when you're back online. Your previous settings are not saved, so you'll need to reconfigure your conversion options.
