Why Use PDF for Your Design Portfolio?

In today's digital world, designers need a professional way to showcase their work while protecting their intellectual property. PDF portfolios offer the perfect balance of presentation quality and security.

Key Portfolio Features

Secure Sharing

Protect your designs with password protection and access controls while sharing with potential clients.

View Tracking

Monitor who views your portfolio and for how long, providing valuable insights into client interest.

Branded Watermarks

Add your personal brand to your portfolio while protecting your work from unauthorized use.

Portfolio Best Practices

1. Portfolio Organization

  • Create a clear project hierarchy
  • Include project descriptions and your role
  • Showcase your best work first
  • Keep the portfolio focused and concise

2. Design Presentation

  • Use high-quality images and mockups
  • Include before/after comparisons
  • Show your design process
  • Highlight key achievements

3. Security Measures

  • Set view limits for sensitive projects
  • Add watermarks with your contact info
  • Prevent unauthorized downloads
  • Track portfolio access

Pro Tip

Create different portfolio versions for different types of clients. Use MaiPDF's access controls to share the appropriate version with each client.

Industry-Specific Portfolio Tips

Graphic Designers

  • Showcase brand identity projects
  • Include print and digital work
  • Demonstrate typography skills

UI/UX Designers

  • Present user flows and wireframes
  • Showcase interactive prototypes
  • Include user research insights

Product Designers

  • Show product development process
  • Include 3D renderings and models
  • Demonstrate material knowledge
