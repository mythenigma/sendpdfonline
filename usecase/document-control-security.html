Published: January 15, 2025 | Updated: December 03, 2025

Welcome to MaiPDF, your trusted platform for secure document management. Learn how to protect your sensitive documents with our advanced control and security features.

Why Document Control Matters

In today's digital world, controlling access to your documents is essential for protecting sensitive information, maintaining compliance, and preventing unauthorized use.

The Risks of Uncontrolled PDF Sharing

Imagine sending a confidential business proposal to a client, only to find it leaked to competitors. This is the reality of uncontrolled PDF sharing. Sensitive information can easily fall into the wrong hands, leading to financial losses and reputational damage.

Common Security Risks

  • Unauthorized document forwarding
  • Uncontrolled access to sensitive information
  • Document leaks and data breaches
  • Loss of business confidentiality
