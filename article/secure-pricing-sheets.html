Introduction to Secure Pricing Sheets

Pricing sheets are critical documents for SaaS and service companies. They often contain sensitive information that must remain confidential to protect your business interests.

Challenges in Sharing Pricing Sheets

When sharing pricing sheets, businesses face several challenges:

  • Unauthorized sharing with competitors.
  • Loss of control over sensitive pricing information.
  • Lack of visibility into who has accessed the document.

Our Advanced Security Features

Our platform offers a range of tools to ensure the secure sharing of pricing sheets:

  • Access Restrictions: Limit access to specific users or domains.
  • View-Only Mode: Prevent downloads and ensure documents are only viewed online.
  • Document Expiration: Set time limits for document access.
  • Analytics: Track views, time spent, and user interactions.

Case Study: Protecting Confidential Pricing

A SaaS company used our platform to share pricing sheets with potential clients. By leveraging our security features, they ensured:

  • Only authorized clients could view the pricing sheets.
  • Access was revoked after the negotiation period.
  • Detailed analytics provided insights into client engagement.
