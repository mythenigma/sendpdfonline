In today's digital world, seamless access to PDF documents is critical. MaiPDF enables easy conversion of PDF files into URL links, making it easy for users to access documents from any device. Additionally, MaiPDF's tracking feature gives you valuable insights into how your documents are being used.

Key Features

  • Universal Accessibility: Convert PDFs into URL links for easy access across devices without requiring additional software.
  • Real-time Access Tracking: Monitor and analyze user interactions with your PDFs, including access times, device types, and locations.
  • Improved Document Management: Track and record access to sensitive documents for better control and security.
