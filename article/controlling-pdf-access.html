Why Control PDF Access?

Have you ever shared a PDF and wondered who might be viewing or forwarding it? In today's fast-paced digital world, keeping track of your documents can be challenging. Whether it's a confidential business proposal or an exclusive training manual, you need to know who's accessing your files and how often.

MaiPDF's view limit feature solves this common problem by giving you simple yet powerful control over your PDF documents. Think of it as a virtual bouncer for your files – you decide exactly how many times each document can be viewed.

Understanding PDF View Limits

MaiPDF makes controlling document access straightforward. You can set a specific number of allowed views (from 1 to 10,000) for any PDF you share. Once that limit is reached, the document automatically locks itself – it's that simple!

  • Get notified when someone views your document
  • See who's accessing your files and when

Simple Yet Powerful

How to Set Up PDF View Limits: A Simple Guide

1. Upload Your PDF

Visit MaiPDF and click the "Upload" button to add your document. It's as simple as dragging and dropping your file.

2. Set Your View Limit

Choose how many times your document can be opened. For example:

  • Set to "1" for one-time viewing (perfect for sensitive documents)
  • Choose "5" for small team reviews
  • Pick "50" for wider distribution with control
  • Use "999999" for unlimited views

3. Add Extra Protection (Optional)

Enhance your document's security with additional features:

  • Password protection - Add a secret key for access
  • Download restrictions - Prevent file saving
  • Print controls - Disable printing options
  • Dynamic watermarks - Track document origin

4. Share Your Protected PDF

Get your secure link and share it with your intended recipients. They can view the document instantly in their browser – no software installation needed!
