Legal Documents

Contracts, NDAs, and privileged communications. Watermarks create a deterrent and an audit trail if confidentiality is breached.

Business Proposals

Pricing documents and strategic plans. If a competitor obtains your proposal, the watermark identifies the leak.

Educational Content

Exam papers and course materials. Watermarked copies deter students from distributing paid or restricted content.

Financial Reports

Investor updates and quarterly reports. Trace who shared non-public financial information.
