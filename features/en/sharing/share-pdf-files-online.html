Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about sharing PDFs with MaiPDF

Is MaiPDF secure for sharing confidential documents?

Yes, MaiPDF uses end-to-end encryption and provides multiple security features including password protection, access controls, and dynamic watermarking to ensure your confidential documents remain secure. We also comply with industry security standards to protect your data.

Do recipients need a MaiPDF account to view shared documents?

No, recipients do not need a MaiPDF account to view shared documents. You can share a secure link or QR code with recipients, and they can access the document directly in their web browser. Optional settings allow you to require email verification or password entry before viewing.

Can I limit how many times a document can be viewed?

Yes, MaiPDF allows you to set view limits for your shared documents. You can specify the maximum number of views, after which the document will no longer be accessible. This feature is particularly useful for sensitive or time-limited offers.

What analytics do I get with MaiPDF document sharing?

MaiPDF provides comprehensive analytics for your shared documents, including number of views, viewer information, time spent on each page, geographic location of viewers, and device types used. These insights help you understand how your documents are being consumed.

Can I prevent downloading or printing of my shared PDFs?

Yes, MaiPDF allows you to disable downloading and printing functionality for your shared documents. Recipients can only view the document in their browser, which helps protect your intellectual property and prevents unauthorized distribution.
