Access Control Features

Multiple Layers of Document Protection

View Count Limits

Set a maximum number of opens (e.g. 1, 5, or 50). Once the limit is hit, the link automatically becomes inactive.

Email Verification Gate

Require viewers to verify their email address before accessing the document. Captures identity in your audit log.

Expiration Dates

Set an auto-expire date. After that date, the link stops working — no manual intervention needed.

Password Protection

Add a password that recipients must enter before viewing your PDF. Only people with the password can access it.

Revoke Access

Disable any shared link instantly from your dashboard. The URL remains but the document becomes inaccessible.

Replace File, Same Link

Upload a new version of your PDF without changing the sharing URL. All recipients automatically see the updated file.

Watermark Protection

Add dynamic watermarks with the viewer's email or custom text to deter screenshots and unauthorized sharing.

Audit Trail

Every view is recorded with timestamp, device, and viewer identity — giving you a complete access history.

Email Verification

Know Exactly Who Reads Your PDF

When email verification is enabled, viewers must enter their email and confirm it before the PDF loads. This gives you a verified record of every person who accessed your document.

Access Layers

Stack Multiple Layers of Protection

# Layer What It Does When to Use
1 Password Requires a password before the PDF loads. Only people who know the password can access it. Confidential docs
2 Email Verify Viewers must verify their email address. Captures identity and creates an audit trail. Contracts & NDAs
3 View Limit Automatically disables the link after a set number of opens (e.g. 1, 5, 10). One-time access
4 Expiration Link stops working after a specific date. No manual cleanup needed. Time-sensitive offers
5 Watermark Overlays viewer-specific text on every page to deter screenshots and redistribution. Portfolios & IP
6 No Download Disables the download button. Viewers can only read in the online viewer. Prevent copies

All layers can be combined on a single document for maximum protection.
