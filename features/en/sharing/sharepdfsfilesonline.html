Complete Control Over Your PDF

One-Click Conversion

Upload your PDF and instantly get a unique, permanent link. No technical steps, no waiting — your secure link is ready in seconds.

View-Only Mode

Prevent recipients from downloading, printing, or copying your PDF. They can only view it in the browser — nothing leaves your control.

Password + Email Gate

Require a password or email address before anyone can view your document. Only authorized recipients gain access.

Expiration Control

Set an expiration date or maximum view count. Once the limit is reached, the link becomes inaccessible — ideal for NDAs and proposals.

Watermark

Apply dynamic watermarks that display the viewer's info on every page. Discourages screenshots and unauthorized redistribution.

Real-Time Analytics

Track every view with timestamps, device types, approximate locations, and browser info. Full audit trail for every document.

Permanent Link

Your link URL stays the same even when you replace the PDF behind it. Update content without redistributing a new link.

Cross-Platform Viewer

Recipients open the PDF in any browser on any device — desktop, tablet, or phone. No app needed, no software to install.
