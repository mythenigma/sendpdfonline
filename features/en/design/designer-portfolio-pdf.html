Portfolio Security Features

Protect your creative work while maintaining a professional presentation

Secure Link Sharing

Share your portfolio via a single secure link. Optionally add a password so only intended recipients can access it. Change or revoke access anytime.

View Analytics

See who viewed your portfolio, when, from what device, and for how long. Use these insights to gauge client interest and optimize your follow-up timing.

Branded Watermarks

Add your studio name, logo, or contact info as a watermark. Protects your work from screenshots while keeping your branding front and center.

Expiration & View Limits

Set your portfolio link to expire after a date or a number of views. Perfect for proposals with deadlines or when sharing with multiple agencies.

Portfolio Best Practices

Tips from across the design industry

Graphic Designers

  • Lead with brand identity projects
  • Include both print and digital work
  • Show typography and color systems
  • Add watermarks to protect comps

UI/UX Designers

  • Present user flows and wireframes
  • Showcase before/after redesigns
  • Include research insights and metrics
  • Set view-only access for prototypes

Product Designers

  • Show the design process, not just results
  • Include 3D renderings and mockups
  • Demonstrate material knowledge
  • Protect manufacturing details with view limits

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I share my design portfolio as a PDF?

PDF preserves fonts, colors, and layout pixel-perfectly across all devices. Unlike website portfolios, PDFs can be shared via secure links with view tracking, access control, and watermark protection — giving you full control over who sees your work and how.

How do I protect my portfolio from being copied?

Use MaiPDF's security features: disable downloads, add watermarks with your name or studio branding, set view limits so the link expires after a set number of opens, and enable password protection. These layers make unauthorized copying significantly harder.

Can I see if a client viewed my portfolio?

Yes. MaiPDF provides real-time view tracking. You'll see when someone opens your portfolio, their location, device type, and how long they viewed it. This helps you time follow-up conversations and gauge interest.

Should I create different portfolios for different clients?

Yes. Tailoring your portfolio to each client's industry increases your chances of winning the project. Use MaiPDF to generate separate secure links for each version, with unique view tracking per link.
