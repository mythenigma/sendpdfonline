View Limit Scenarios

1-3 Views
One-Time Review

Contracts, proposals, and quotes shared for a single review. Ensures the recipient reads once and can't forward endlessly.

5-10 Views
Team Review

Documents shared within a small team. Enough views for everyone, but limits viral sharing beyond the group.

50-100 Views
Department Distribution

Policies, manuals, or training materials shared across a department with a reasonable access cap.

Time-Based
Expiration Date

Combine view limits with expiry dates. Documents become inaccessible after a specific date regardless of remaining views.
