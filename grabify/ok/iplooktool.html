Track Your Links with Grabify

Grabify is a powerful IP tracking tool that allows you to shorten links and track important analytics like the location, device type, browser, and more of the users who click on your links.

How Grabify Works

To start tracking, simply follow these steps:

  1. Enter a URL in the provided input box.
  2. Click the "Shorten" button to create a unique Grabify link.
  3. Share the shortened link on your desired platform.
  4. Visit the analytics page to see data like IP address, location, and more of the users who clicked your link.

IP Lookup Tool

Type an IP in the search box to find information.

What Is IP Logging?

IP logging involves tracking the IP addresses of users who click on a specific link. When users click the Grabify-generated link, their IP address, along with information about their device, browser, and location, is recorded. This can be useful for monitoring engagement, tracking users' geographic locations, or even troubleshooting network issues.

What Results Will You See?

When you track a link, you will receive details about the click, including:

  • IP Address
  • Location (Country, City)
  • Device Type (Mobile, Desktop, etc.)
  • Browser and Operating System
