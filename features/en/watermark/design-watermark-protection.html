Why Protect Your Design Work?

In the digital age, protecting your creative work is crucial. Watermarking and other security features help prevent unauthorized use while maintaining the professional presentation of your designs.

Advanced Protection Features

Dynamic Watermarks

Add personalized watermarks that include viewer information, date, and time to track document usage.

Access Control

Control who can view, download, or print your designs with granular permission settings.

Usage Tracking

Monitor how your designs are being viewed and by whom, providing valuable insights into client interest.

Watermarking Best Practices

1. Watermark Placement

  • Position watermarks strategically to protect key design elements
  • Use semi-transparent watermarks that don't obscure the design
  • Consider different watermark styles for different types of work
  • Include your contact information in watermarks

2. Security Settings

  • Set view limits for sensitive projects
  • Enable download prevention for view-only access
  • Add expiration dates to temporary access
  • Implement password protection for confidential work

3. Brand Protection

  • Use your brand colors in watermarks
  • Include your logo or brand mark
  • Maintain consistent watermarking across all projects
  • Create different watermark styles for different client types

Pro Tip

Create a watermark template that includes your brand elements, contact information, and viewer details. This ensures consistent protection across all your design work.
