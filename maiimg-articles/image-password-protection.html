Why I Use Password Protection for Images

I used to share image links without any protection. Then I realized anyone with the link could access my images - even if I only wanted specific people to see them. Password protection changed everything.

Control Access

Only people with the password can view your images. Share the password separately through email, message, or in-person. This ensures only intended recipients can access your content.

Prevent Unauthorized Access

Even if someone gets your image link, they can't view the images without the password. This prevents accidental sharing or unauthorized access from forwarded links.

Professional Use

For client portfolios or sensitive business images, password protection adds an extra layer of security. Clients feel more confident knowing their content is protected.

Privacy Control

Perfect for personal photos, family albums, or private collections. You control exactly who can see your images by controlling who has the password.

3

Share Link & Password

Best Practices for Password Protection

Strong Passwords

  • Use at least 8 characters
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid common words or personal information
  • Use different passwords for different image sets

Secure Sharing

  • Never include password in the same message as the link
  • Share password through separate channel (email, phone, in-person)
  • Use different passwords for different recipients
  • Change password if link is compromised
