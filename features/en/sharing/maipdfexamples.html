Use Cases

How Professionals Use MaiPDF

Sales Proposals

Share pricing proposals with clients using password protection and view tracking. Know instantly when a prospect opens your proposal and follow up at the right time.

Legal Contracts

Distribute NDAs, agreements, and court filings with expiration dates and watermarks. Maintain an audit trail of every access for compliance purposes.

HR Documents

Share employee handbooks, benefit guides, and onboarding packets securely. Set view limits so documents are only accessible during the onboarding period.

Investor Reports

Share quarterly earnings, board decks, and financial summaries with password-protected links. Expiration dates ensure outdated figures are no longer accessible.

Training Materials

Distribute training manuals and certification guides to employees or students. Watermarks with the viewer's name discourage unauthorized redistribution.

Marketing Collateral

Share brochures, catalogs, and media kits via link or QR code. Replace files when content is updated — the same link always shows the latest version.

Academic Papers

Share research papers and thesis drafts with peer reviewers. View limits and expiration dates prevent widespread distribution of unpublished work.

Design Portfolios

Share your portfolio with potential clients or employers via a clean link. Watermarks protect your creative work while analytics show who reviewed your designs.
