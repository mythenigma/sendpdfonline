Image Sharing Link

Turn images into links you can control after you share them.

Why use image links?

Links are faster than sending files and let you adjust access even after you hit send.

3‑step workflow

  1. Upload your images.
  2. Set access rules (view limits, expiration, download control).
  3. Share the link or QR code.

Controls you get

  • View limits
  • Expiration dates
  • Disable downloads
  • One‑click disable

Best use cases

  • Client images & quotes
  • Campaign assets
  • Internal collaboration
