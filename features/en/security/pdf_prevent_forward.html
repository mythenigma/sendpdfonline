How to Prevent PDF Files from Being Forwarded

Published: January 15, 2025 | Updated: December 03, 2025

MaiPDF helps you control PDF file access by setting limits on the number of views, preventing copying and printing, and even blocking forwarding. This tool is perfect for protecting your document's content.

How PDF Protection Works

Animation: Unauthorized users are blocked while verified recipients can access the protected PDF

MaiPDF Security Features

View Limit Control

Limit the number of times a PDF can be opened to prevent unauthorized sharing. Once the limit is reached, the link becomes inactive.

Prevent Downloading

Block users from downloading your PDF. They can only view it online, ensuring your content stays under your control.

Disable Printing

Prevent recipients from printing your PDF document, keeping your content protected from physical distribution.

Email Notifications

See when and where the PDF file is opened using email notifications. Get alerted every time someone views your document.

Email Verification

Require recipients to verify their email before accessing the PDF. Only authorized viewers can see your content.

Dynamic Watermark

Add dynamic watermarks showing viewer's email or IP address. Discourages screenshots and unauthorized sharing.
