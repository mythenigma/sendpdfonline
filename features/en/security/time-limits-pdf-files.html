MaiPDF Security Settings

MaiPDF provides 5 powerful settings to control how your PDF is accessed and shared:

  • Views - Limit the number of times your PDF can be opened
  • Verify - Require email verification before viewing
  • Download - Allow or prevent PDF downloads
  • Watermark - Add visible watermarks for protection
  • Alert - Get notified when someone views your PDF

Why View Limits Matter

View limits are essential for:

  • Confidential Documents: Ensure sensitive information is only viewed a limited number of times
  • Trial Content: Allow preview access before requiring purchase
  • Exclusive Access: Control distribution of premium content
  • Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements for document access control
