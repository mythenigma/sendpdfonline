Access Records

See Exactly Who Viewed Your Document

When a client opens your shared PDF, MaiPDF captures a detailed access record automatically. Navigate to your control panel and click on the document to view the full log of visits.

Each record shows the viewer's email (if verification is enabled), the exact date and time, IP address, browser, and operating system. Use these insights to identify engaged prospects, confirm delivery, and time your follow-up perfectly.
