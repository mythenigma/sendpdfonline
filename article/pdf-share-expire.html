What are Auto-Expiring PDF Links?

Auto-expiring PDF links are secure sharing links that automatically become invalid after a specified period. This feature is essential for protecting sensitive information and ensuring that documents are only accessible for a limited time. With MaiPDF, you can set expiration times ranging from minutes to months, giving you complete control over document access.

How to Create Auto-Expiring PDF Links

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF's secure platform
  2. Navigate to the sharing settings
  3. Enable the expiration feature
  4. Set your desired expiration time
  5. Configure additional security options
  6. Generate and share your secure link
