Track PDF Usage

Published: January 15, 2025 | Updated: December 03, 2025

Welcome to MaiPDF, the ultimate platform for secure and efficient PDF management. Discover how our tracking features can provide valuable insights into your document usage.

Why Track PDF Usage?

Tracking PDF usage helps you understand how your documents are being accessed and utilized. This is crucial for improving engagement, ensuring compliance, and protecting sensitive information.

MaiPDF's Advanced Tracking Features

MaiPDF offers state-of-the-art tracking capabilities, including:

  • Real-time access logs
  • Viewer location and device details
  • Time spent on each page
  • Download and sharing analytics

How to Enable Tracking

  1. Upload your PDF to MaiPDF.
  2. Enable the tracking feature in the settings.
  3. Share the document link with your audience.
  4. Monitor usage through the MaiPDF dashboard.

Benefits of Tracking PDF Usage

  • Gain insights into user behavior.
  • Enhance document security and control.
  • Optimize content for better engagement.
